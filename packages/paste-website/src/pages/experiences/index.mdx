## What is an experience?

Experiences, like [patterns](/patterns) and [page templates](/page-templates), bring together a collection of [components](/components) to provide UI solutions for specific use cases. Experiences, however, provide guidelines for common flows and UIs that span entire products or product suites. Experiences are for broader use cases than patterns and page templates, which typically exist within a single page or contained flow.

Paste experiences combine comprehensive UI kits with extensive guidance and realistic examples to help you build your product and drive consistency across the company.

For more information about how Paste is built and where experiences fit into that hierarchy, read [About Paste](/introduction/about-paste#whats-a-design-system).
